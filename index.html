muses: conscious fashion studio is a forward-thinking non-profit building an extensive apparel production training program for low-income immigrant and refugee communities in Seattle. We focus their existing skills to meet the rapidly growing demand for local, ethical, and high-quality apparel manufacturing services.

Nurturing local talents stemming from around the globe, Muses revives the fine art of creating handcrafted, “Made in Seattle” apparel.

Join us in addressing this social need and market demand as we bring a sustainable face to apparel manufacturing.
