    GREEN EILEEN

    GREEN EILEEN is a recycled clothing initiative of EILEEN FISHER INC. At GREEN EILEEN we resell gently worn EILEEN FISHER clothing at an affordable price. All profits from resale are donated to programs that support positive, systematic change for women and girls around the world. Clothing that cannot be resold in our store are donated to women in need or upcycled to create new value items.  

    Testimonial

    Muses Conscious Fashion Studio offers technical expertise, an agile business philosophy, and social consciousness at its core. These are the qualities that GREEN EILEEN looked for in a partner to support our new upcycling initiatives. With Muses help in pattern making, cut and sew, and talent scouting, GREEN EILEEN is launching an upcycled clothing line and continuing to innovate for a more responsible apparel manufacturing industry.




    BYDFAULT

    One Artist. One Cause. One Shirt. Located in Redmond, WA, BYDFAULT is a socially conscious clothing company that combines fashion and art to improve people’s lives. BYDFAULT sells premium clothing with social cause-inspired designs from contemporary artists. BYDFAULT donates $5 of every sale to the cause that inspired the design. BYDFAULT items are manufactured responsibly and sold online in select retailers.
