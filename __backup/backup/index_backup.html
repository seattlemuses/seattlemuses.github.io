muses is a Seattle-based non-profit striving to equip low-income immigrant and refugees with skills and ethical job opportunities in apparel production, while increasing fashion designers’ access to high-quality, local, and responsible manufacturing services.

We nurture local talents and revive the fine art of creating high-quality, handcrafted, and “made in Seattle” apparel.

Join us in addressing this social need and market demand as we bring a sustainable face to apparel manufacturing
